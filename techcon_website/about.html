Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small gathering of innovators and engineers in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a premier global conference attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. Each year, TechCon brings together leaders in technology to share ideas, launch projects, and inspire the next generation of innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking within the tech community. Our mission is to spotlight groundbreaking technologies, promote ethical innovation, and empower voices from diverse backgrounds to shape the future of technology.
